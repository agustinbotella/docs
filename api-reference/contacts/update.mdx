---
title: Update Contact
api: POST /contacts/update
description: Update a contact's status and/or campaign by phone number.
---

Updates a contact identified by their phone number. You can change their status, move them to a different campaign, or both. At least one of `status` or `campaignId` must be provided.

<Note>
  Use [List Statuses](/api-reference/contacts/statuses) to retrieve all valid status values for your team before making update requests.
</Note>

## Request Body

<ParamField body="phone" type="string" required>
  The phone number of the contact to update, in E.164 format (e.g., `+11234567890`).
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="status" type="string">
  New status to assign to the contact. At least one of `status` or `campaignId` is required.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="campaignId" type="string (UUID)">
  New campaign to move the contact to. At least one of `status` or `campaignId` is required.
</ParamField>

## Behavior Notes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Setting a terminal status">
    Changing a contact's status to a terminal status (e.g., `fulfilled`, `DNC`, `paused`) will automatically **cancel all scheduled calls, SMS, and WhatsApp messages** for that contact.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Fulfilling a contact">
    Setting `status` to `fulfilled` automatically sets the `fulfilledAt` timestamp to the current time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Changing campaigns">
    Moving a contact to a new campaign will cancel all existing scheduled items tied to the old campaign and reschedule them according to the new campaign's configuration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Response

<ResponseField name="success" type="boolean">
  Always `true` on a successful request.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="message" type="string">
  Human-readable confirmation message.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="contact" type="object">
  The updated contact object.

  <Expandable title="Contact fields">
    <ResponseField name="id" type="string (UUID)">
      Contact unique identifier.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="phoneNumber" type="string">
      Phone number in E.164 format.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="name" type="string">
      Contact name.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="status" type="string">
      Updated contact status.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="campaignId" type="string (UUID)">
      Current campaign ID.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="fulfilledAt" type="string or null">
      Timestamp when the contact was fulfilled. Set automatically when status is changed to `fulfilled`.
    </ResponseField>
    <ResponseField name="updatedAt" type="string (ISO 8601)">
      Timestamp of this update.
    </ResponseField>
  </Expandable>
</ResponseField>
